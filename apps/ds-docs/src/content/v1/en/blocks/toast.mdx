## Behavior

- **Auto-close**: The progress bar decreases over the given `durationMs`; when it reaches zero, the toast closes.
- **Close button**: Users can dismiss the toast at any time.
- **Action**: The action link/button can navigate or trigger a retry without closing the toast (handled by your app).

<Callout type="tip" title="Accessibility">
Ensure the close button has an `aria-label` and the progress bar uses `role="progressbar"` with `aria-valuenow`, `aria-valuemin`, and `aria-valuemax`.
</Callout>
